# Text-to-Speech Reader by Aleksi

An accessible tool to read long articles or blog posts out loud.

## Chrome Web Store Listing Details

### Store Listing

**Title:** Text-to-Speech Reader by Aleksi

**Short Description:** An accessible tool to read long articles or blog posts out loud.

**Detailed Description:**
> Give your eyes a break! The Text-to-Speech Reader by Aleksi allows you to highlight any text on a webpage and have it read aloud to you instantly. Whether it's a long blog post, a news article, or study materials, simply open the popup, adjust your reading speed, and hit play. Accessibilty and multi-tasking have never been easier.

**Category:** Accessibility (or Productivity)

**Language:** English (United States)

---

### Privacy Practices

**Single Purpose:**
> The single purpose of this extension is to provide an accessible text-to-speech utility that reads highlighted text aloud to the user.

**Permission Justification:**
> - `activeTab` / `scripting`: Needed to access the currently highlighted text on the active webpage so the extension knows what to read.
> - `tts`: Required to use Chrome's native Text-to-Speech engine to convert the text into spoken audio.

**Data Usage:**
> This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any user data. The text read by the TTS engine is processed locally within the browser. It complies fully with Chrome's Limited Use policy.
